|---|---|---|

| 12 -> 13 | Family and institutions realign under total stress. | Rescue and survival failure of chapter 12 forces a full coalition and tactical integration. |
| 13 -> 14 | Thematic pressure compresses into one final ethical decision. | Final anchor timeline forces direct trade-off between procedural strategy and immediate protection action. |
| 14 -> 15 | Resolution binds all four threads together. | Ritual collapse and custody/repair sequences restore belonging and family-first safety while correcting failures. |
